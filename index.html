Flight Booking

I spearheaded the development and implementation of a new airline flight booking system utilizing ServiceNow's portal. This innovative system empowers passengers to manage their travel seamlessly, offering functionalities for booking flights, checking in for upcoming trips, cancelling reservations if needed, and tracking the status of their flights in real-time. This project streamlines the travel experience for passengers, reduces reliance on manual processes, and improves overall efficiency for the airline.
